Defeat Belmax Court!

Attention citizens of Saint Andrews!

Belmax Court development will negatively affect your quality of life.

A proposal has been approved in theory by council (Dec. 13, 2021) to massively upzone 200 Mowat Drive to allow 15 small condo townhomes crammed into a 2 acre parcel. Currently, this parcel is zoned as estate residential, the plan is to upzone the land to high density multiple residential. The only access to this land is through a right-of-way through adjacent properties. Imagine 30+ cars going through your property daily?!

Allowing this development will remove value from your homes and community, and give it to out of town developers.

There will be an electronic hearing of objections meeting on January 10th at 7pm.
