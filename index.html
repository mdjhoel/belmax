Defeat Belmax Court!

Attention citizens of Saint Andrews!

Belmax Court development temporarily defeated!

The proposal to upzone 200B Mowat Drive to allow 15 small condo townhomes crammed into a 2 acre parcel was unamimously defeated by the Planning Advisory Committee early January 2022. Currently, this parcel is zoned as estate residential and the only access is a right of way through 200 Mowat Drive.

Belmax will likely resubmit another proposal to develop this landlocked property.
